General Tips for All Articles

Structure Your Content

Start with a Subtitle that matches your title, then use Headers to organize sections. Each section can contain Paragraphs with explanations and Code Blocks with examples.

Keep It Readable

Don't create super long paragraphs. Break them up with headers and code examples. Aim for paragraphs that are 3-5 sentences maximum.

Use Categories Wisely

Select 1-3 categories that best describe your article. Too many categories dilutes the classification, but too few makes it hard to find. For example, an article could be both "Programming" AND "Web Development", but probably shouldn't also be tagged with "DevOps" unless it specifically covers that.

Image Selection

Use professional images or placeholders. The image appears in article listings, so choose something eye-catching. Services like Unsplash, Pexels, or Placeholder.com offer free images.

URL Slugs

Keep slugs short and descriptive. Use hyphens to separate words, all lowercase. Examples: intro-to-nodejs, react-hooks-guide, best-practices-2024
